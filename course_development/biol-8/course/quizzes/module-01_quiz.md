## Part A: Multiple Choice (7 points)
*Choose the best answer for each question. (1 point each)*

1. Which of the following is a characteristic of ALL living things?
   - A) Photosynthesis
   - B) Movement
   - C) Made of cells
   - D) Sexual reproduction

2. The correct order of the steps in the scientific method is:
   - A) Hypothesis → Observation → Experiment → Conclusion
   - B) Observation → Hypothesis → Experiment → Conclusion
   - C) Experiment → Observation → Hypothesis → Conclusion
   - D) Conclusion → Hypothesis → Observation → Experiment

3. Which level of organization is more complex than a tissue?
   - A) Cell
   - B) Molecule
   - C) Organ
   - D) Organelle

4. A well-tested explanation that unifies a broad range of observations is called a:
   - A) Hypothesis
   - B) Theory
   - C) Variable
   - D) Control

5. Homeostasis refers to:
   - A) The ability to reproduce
   - B) Maintaining a stable internal environment
   - C) Response to light
   - D) Growth and development

6. In an experiment testing whether fertilizer affects plant growth, the control group would be:
   - A) Plants given fertilizer
   - B) Plants given water but no fertilizer
   - C) Plants given extra sunlight
   - D) Plants grown in different soil types

7. Which of the following represents the correct order from smallest to largest?
   - A) Cell → Tissue → Organ → Organism
   - B) Tissue → Cell → Organ → Organism
   - C) Organ → Tissue → Cell → Organism
   - D) Organism → Organ → Tissue → Cell
